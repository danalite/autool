What you can do with AuTool
Workspace quick setup
Arrange windows to different positions? hide or show desktop icons? switch monitor input source, and more. All with a single click!
Monitor important things
Track time spent on each app; Parse your order emails to record your spending or delivery status, etc.
Schedule and hotkey
Trigger a script to a hotkey, or schedule it to run it later. You can even let it run remotely on a server.
Integrate AI into your workflow
You can use AuTool to send window captures or local files to remote AI processing, e.g., text recognition, object detection.
Quick knowledge flashcard
Import your notes or cheat sheets into a AuTool script, and you can quickly refresh your mind by triggering a notification.
Glue everything together
Automatically send a notification to Slack when a new email comes in? Sure, AuTool can do it for you!
An editing experience content editors will love
Provide the ease of use and flexibility that your content editors crave for. Rely on our user-friendly default blocks or use plug&play editor components to build great experiences.
